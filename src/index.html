Passion for web development

It all started in October 2018 with an internship. That’s when I decided to build a website for my undergraduate project, and I did!

From hobby to career

After completing my degree, I continued experimenting, fueled by my passion for learning and creating new projects. In 2023, I finally realized that web development is what I truly want to pursue for the rest of my life.

My Journey Through Web Development

At first, I built websites with databases, captivated by the idea of storing and processing data. Later, I shifted my focus to designing user interfaces, inspired by topics that fueled my creativity. As I began The Odin Project courses, the path ahead became clearer—I could feel myself growing with each new project.

My Achievements

I’ve developed dozens of templates, 15 JavaScript applications, and 7 websites with databases. My front-end expertise includes HTML, CSS, JavaScript, MDBootstrap, and Bootstrap. On the back-end, I’ve worked with PHP and MySQL, handling small projects successfully. I’m also proficient with SCSS, JSON, Webpack, and Vite. While not an expert, I’m very comfortable with Git and GitHub, and I regularly use npm and the command line.

My Goal

My primary goal is to continuously grow and learn—perseverance and consistency are the foundation of success in any field. I am looking to collaborate with a team where I can both contribute and learn from innovative projects. The right environment combined with the right mindset equals accelerated progress.

My Story

I've always liked computers, but football fueled my passion for web development.

My greatest passion is sports, especially football. From a young age, I developed a strong love for mathematics and computer science. I knew from my early teenage years that I wanted to become a 'computer scientist.'

In 2015, I enrolled in a specialized faculty (IETI, Calculatoare), graduating in 2019. The latter part of 2018 was pivotal in my journey, as it solidified my decision to pursue a career in web development. I continued my studies and developed as a programmer. Dynamic websites fascinated me, especially data storage and processing. Throughout my journey, inspired by various topics, I began to appreciate the visual aspects more and more, which motivated me to build more and more templates.

Everything went well until the second half of 2021 when I started to lose my way. The year 2022 was not a year of progress, but it helped me rediscover myself and understand what I want from life. Starting in 2023, things began to improve. I managed to overcome obstacles and stay on the path I had chosen. And the year 2024 has come to strengthen this conviction.
